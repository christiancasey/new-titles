The ISAW Library compiles a single Zotero "library" for the entire year, with each month representing a separate Zotero "collection." The metadata tags are only linked or searchable at the "library" level, not at the "collection" level. For example, if you review acquisitions for December 2015 and select the tag for "Hittite," nothing may come up. However, if you move up the hierarchy from the collection "December 2015" to the "library" for 2015 and then click on "Hittite," you will find items tagged from previous months.
